Our Framework

Six Pillars
of Vanguard Sourcing

We don't just broker transactions; we provide a structural framework of trade certainty. These six pillars guarantee logistics and purity.

01

Traceable Origin Sourcing

Every cargo is trace-coded back to verified agricultural cooperatives, certified mines, or quality yarn spinner mills. Trust begins in the source.

02

Independent Lab Auditing

Purity levels, granularity, moisture levels, and tensile strength curves are tested by third-party auditors (SGS/Bureau Veritas) prior to container seal.

03

Specification-Matched Cargo

Every container shipment is customized to match your processing parameters, matching exact grade targets without standard variance.

04

Trade Corridor Expertise

Technical routing, cargo insurance, marine freight coordination, and multi-country customs clearance managed seamlessly by our local logistics teams.

05

Regulatory Compliance Packages

Phytosanitary clearances, GOTS organic certifications, safety sheets, and custom document declarations standard for every delivery.

06

Absolute Price Transparency

Clear commercial margins, direct sourcing options, and zero cargo replacements. You receive exactly the cargo parameters you ordered.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Expert FAQ

What commodities does Vanguard Global Trade specialise in?

+
We specialize in three main trading divisions: agro-commodities (organic spices, rice, tea), sustainable minerals (high-purity silica sand, kaolin clay, bentonite), and organic textiles (GOTS certified yarn and jute fibres).

Do you provide cargo certification documents?

+
Yes. Every container is accompanied by a custom certification pack including independent Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from SGS/Bureau Veritas, Phytosanitary clearance, GOTS organic certification when applicable, and complete custom logistics packages.

Do you supply custom specifications or samples?

+
Yes. Pre-shipment samples are dispatched via DHL/FedEx for technical approval. We match specification sheets tailored to your manufacturing requirements.

What shipping incoterms do you support?

+
We support standard international incoterms including FOB (primary ports in India), CIF, and CFR. Freight coordination and insurance are handled standard.
